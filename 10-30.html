Wilderness First Aid (WFA) Certification [sat 11/8 - sun 11/9]

Ever wanted to know how to splint a broken bone with a crazy creek? Roll your buddy up into a human burrito with a tarp to protect him/her from hypothermia?

Now’s your chance! Sign up for SOLO's Wilderness First Aid (WFA) certification course, sponsored by Yale Outdoors! WFA certification will teach you fundamental first aid skills, so when you venture into the great outdoors you’ll know how to effectively respond to/treat basic injuries and illnesses.

The course is $180, but subsidized down to $155 for Yalies, and will take place Sat-Sun November 8-9, from 9am-5pm on both days. There is also an optional CPR certification component from 6pm-8pm on Saturday for an additional $35. A limited number of partial scholarships are available for those who can demonstrate financial need.

If you have any questions or would like to apply for a scholarship, email YO’s Wilderness Med Coordinator Emma Spence at emma.spence@yale.edu. Sign up here!

Sunrise Biking! [friday 10/31]

After restful Thursday night last week, join Yale Outdoors as we bike to West Rock this Friday to watch the beautiful sunrise yet again. This trip will require a bike, so bring your own, borrow a friends, or see about Zagster (sometimes a little sketchy). If you are in need of a bike, let us know and we'll see what we can do, but no guarantees. Please bring a front and rear light. Even flashlights will do. In addition, at the top of West Rock, there will be optional Yoga, headed up by Yale Outdoors' own Tessa Adler. Feel free to bring a mat if you want, or get better acquainted with the Earth. We'll leave at 05:45 and be back by 08:30. Email angus.mossman@yale.edu or tessa.adler@yale.edu with questions. Sign up [here].
