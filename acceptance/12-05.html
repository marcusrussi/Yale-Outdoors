WinterYO: Sleeping Giant Day Hike[12/16]

Stuck on campus in between finishing finals and heading away for break? Join us for one last hike on Sleeping Giant! What better way to celebrate finishing the semester than with some fresh air and gorgeous views? Pack a lunch (you can ask the dining hall for one) and we'll head out at 10am and return by 4pm. This will be on the tougher side of hikes, so bring some sturdy boots! Whether it's covered in snow or still sunny, it should be beautiful! Sign up on the website or email sofia.carrera@yale.edu and christina.wang@yale.edu. And, if you have a car to offer, let us know!

WinterYO: Berkshires Overnight[1/9-1/10]

What better finale to your winter break than exploring the beautiful Berkshires in Massachusetts? Camped out in a Bell Tent - a type of canvas tent used by the British Army in the 19th century - we will stay at the Beartown State Reservation and do some light hiking or snowshoeing in the area. We will depart from campus midday on Friday, January 9th and return the afternoon of the 10th, leaving you all of Sunday to mentally prepare for the adventures of a new semester. The trip is $15 for members and $25 for non-members. It will be very cold, so make sure to bring plenty of warm layers and appropriate winter gear so that you can stay cozy and dry while wandering in the woods!
